Why this project?

I am interested in Deep Learning and (of course) I have been reading lot of papers on this topic. However, it is very difficult to keep up with all the publications that are released daily.
To focus on the most important publications, I have been using the arXiv website and manually selecting the papers that are relevant to me. This process is very tedious and time-consuming.
I have thought of automating this process and hence I came up with the idea of Deep Learning Updates. This is a Telegram Channel where I post daily summaries of research papers in the field of Natural Language Processing (cs.CL), Computer Vision (cs.CV), Audio Signal Processing (cs.SD) and Multimedia (cs.MM).

How it works?

After an initial labeling process, all the papers that are posted on arXiv every day are automatically ranked according to their estimated relevance. The abstract of each selected paper is further summarized to generate a TL;DR snippet. The summaries are published on the Telegram channel every day.
Whenever available, the code of the paper is also highlighted in the daily digest.
